A helping hand in community cleanliness.

Alert the community of litter simply by posting an image.

This is Pick of the Litter, a free, mobile application created with the community in mind. See a piece of litter that you cannot remove? Cue the community. Post a picture of the litter with Pick of the Litter and watch the community swoop in to assist. Simple as that. When you post a picture of litter it viewable to everyone using the app within 25 miles. Allowing someone to come and remove the litter in question.
